"hey, I wonder if I can make a website."

So a quick Google and a stop to W3School HTML and I was off. cout >> "This is my First Webpage"; Thats where my problem started! As an artistic person with an OCD nature for visually appleasing sights, I couldnt not leave it as a black and white... soo i moved to a CSS tutorial and began looking at website i thought looked nice and checked out what they had behind the sense.
